The Game

Imagine 500 people got together to play a game.

Each peson starts with $100 (everyone starts off equal). Every round, each person has to walk up to a random person and flip a coin. If they win the coin toss, they get 10% of the other person's money, if they lose the coin toss they have to give up 10% of their own money.
